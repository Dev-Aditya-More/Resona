# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** July 3, 2026

## Resona Privacy Policy

Resona is committed to protecting your privacy. This application is designed to function primarily as an offline media player and does not collect, store, or sell your personal information.

## Information We Collect

Resona does **not** collect any personally identifiable information such as:

- Name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Location
- Contacts
- Messages

## Permissions

Depending on your Android version, Resona may request the following permissions:

### Storage / Media Access

Used solely to:

- Import audio files selected by you.
- Display and play music stored on your device.
- Build your playback queue and music library.

Your files remain on your device and are **never uploaded** anywhere.

## Data Storage

Any app preferences such as:

- Theme
- Playback settings
- Queue information
- Recently played songs

are stored locally on your device.

No data is transmitted to external servers.

## Internet Connection

Resona does not require an internet connection for its core functionality.

If future versions introduce online features (such as cloud backup, lyrics, analytics, or updates), this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

## Third-Party Services

At this time, Resona does **not** use third-party services that collect user data, including:

- Analytics
- Advertising SDKs
- Crash reporting services

If any third-party services are added in future releases, they will be disclosed in this policy.

## Children's Privacy

Resona is not directed toward children under the age of 13. The application does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone.

## Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as new features are introduced. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
